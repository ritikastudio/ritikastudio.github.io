FAQ

Good to know before you reach out

Is the founding offer actually free?

Yes — the Brand Starter package is free for the first 10 founding clients. In exchange, we ask for an honest testimonial and permission to feature the work once it's done.

How long does a project take?

Most Brand Starter projects take 1–2 weeks from our first conversation to final files, depending on how quickly we get feedback at each step.

What do I need to provide to get started?

Just a short conversation about your business and what you're picturing. No brief or paperwork required — we figure it out together.

What happens after the founding spots are gone?

Pricing moves to standard rates shown in the Services section above. Founding clients keep their free work regardless.

Do you work with businesses outside India?

Yes — Moon Glade works with founders and businesses anywhere, fully remote.
